Mac n' Cheese

  • Time: 8-10 mins
  • Serves: 2-3 People
Ingredients Quantity
Cheese Packet 1
Mac N' Cheese 1
Stick of butter 1/2

Step 1: Take out the Mac N' Cheese shells and put them in a bowl.

Step 2: Bring water to a boiling point

Step 3: Put the shells into the boiling water and mix till soft

Step 4: Strain the water out of the noodles and put in a bowl

Step 5: Mix cheese packet in with noodles

Step 6: Serve and enjoy:)

Avocado toast

  • Time: 3 Minutes
  • Serves: 1 Person
Ingrediants Quantity
Loaf of bread 1
Avocado 1
Salt A sprinkle

Step 1: Cut two slices of bread from the loaf of bread

Step 2: Put the slices in the toaster

Step 3: While the toast is toasting, get a knife and cut the avocado in half,
take out the pit and now you should have 2 avocado halfs.

Step 4:Take the toast out of the toaster, and spread the avocado on the toast,
as if you were rubbing butter on your toast.

Step 5: You can sprinkle some salt if you'd like. Enjoy!
